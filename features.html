Foodle Features

Discover how Foodle transforms your food discovery experience

Social Food Sharing

Share your culinary adventures with friends and followers through beautiful posts, stories, and collections.

  • Photo-rich food posts with location tagging
  • 24-hour food stories
  • Create themed collections (Best Burgers, Date Night Spots, etc.)
  • Follow friends and food influencers

Dish-Level Reviews

Unlike traditional restaurant reviews, Foodle lets you review specific dishes so you know exactly what to order.

  • Rate individual dishes, not just restaurants
  • Photo verification of reviews
  • Flavor profile tagging
  • Price-to-satisfaction rating

Food Maps & Exploration

Discover culinary hotspots around you or plan your food adventures for your next trip.

  • Interactive food maps
  • Neighborhood food guides
  • Travel planning for foodies
  • Hidden gem highlights

Personalized Recommendations

Our smart algorithm learns your taste preferences and dietary needs to suggest dishes and restaurants you'll actually love.

  • Preference-based discovery feed
  • Dietary restriction filtering (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
  • Cuisine and flavor profile matching
  • Budget-friendly options filter

Restaurant Connection

Follow your favorite restaurants for updates, specials, and events directly in your feed.

  • Restaurant profiles with menu highlights
  • Special event announcements
  • New dish alerts
  • Chef spotlights and stories
