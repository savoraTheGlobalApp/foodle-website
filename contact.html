Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

I'm interested in partnering with Foodle. Who should I contact?

Please use our contact form and select "Partnership Opportunity" as the subject. Our partnerships team will get back to you promptly.

Do you offer support for technical issues?

Yes! For any technical issues or questions about using the app, please select "Support" in the contact form subject line.

I have feedback about the app. How can I share it?

We love hearing from our users! Please select "Feedback" in the contact form, and your thoughts will be shared directly with our product team.
