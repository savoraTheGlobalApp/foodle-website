Frequently Asked Questions

When will Foodle officially launch?

We're planning to launch our beta version in Q3 2023, with a full public launch in Q4 2023. Waitlist members will get access in order of signup, so the earlier you join, the sooner you'll get access!

How does the waitlist work?

Once you sign up, you'll be placed in our queue. We'll be sending invites in batches, starting with our earliest supporters. You can move up the waitlist by referring friends to sign up using your unique referral link.

What platforms will Foodle be available on?

Foodle will launch on iOS and Android simultaneously, with a web version coming shortly after the initial mobile app launch.

Will Foodle be available in my city?

We're launching initially in major US cities, with plans to expand quickly to other regions. The information you provide about your location will help us prioritize our expansion.

Is there a cost to join the waitlist?

No, joining the waitlist is completely free. We'll also be offering special founding member perks to our early supporters when we launch.

I'm a restaurant owner. How can I get my restaurant on Foodle?

We'd love to have you! Please indicate that you're a restaurant owner when signing up for the waitlist, and we'll be in touch with specific information about our restaurant partnership program.
